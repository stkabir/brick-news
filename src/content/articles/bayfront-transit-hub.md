Brickell steps into a new era of mobility as cranes arrive at Bayfront Park. The $420M project unites Metrorail, Metromover, and express bus lines under one sleek glass canopy with shaded plazas for daytime comfort. Local retailers are already vying for the 35,000 square feet of pop-up-ready space that will ring the central concourse.
